We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients through transparency, reliability, and exceptional service. Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical roof installation take?

Most residential roofing projects are completed within 1-3 days, depending on the size and complexity of your roof. Our experienced team works efficiently while maintaining the highest quality standards.

What warranty coverage do you offer?

We provide a comprehensive warranty package including 25-year coverage on materials and 10-year coverage on workmanship. All warranties are fully transferable and backed by our commitment to customer satisfaction.

Do you help with insurance claims?

Yes, we have extensive experience working with insurance companies and can assist you throughout the claims process. Our team provides detailed documentation and works directly with adjusters to ensure a smooth experience.

What roofing materials do you recommend?

We offer a wide range of high-quality materials including asphalt shingles, metal roofing, and synthetic slate. Our experts will help you choose the best option based on your budget, style preferences, and climate considerations.

How do you ensure safety during installation?

Safety is our top priority. We maintain full insurance coverage, follow strict safety protocols, and ensure all team members are certified and properly trained. We also protect your property with comprehensive site preparation and cleanup.
