5 Critical Signs Your Calgary Roof Needs Replacement

Calgary's extreme weather conditions can severely impact your roof's lifespan. Five critical signs indicate the need for roof replacement: age-related deterioration (typically 20-25 years), visible shingle damage including curling and missing shingles, interior water damage manifesting as stains or mold, energy efficiency issues leading to increased heating costs, and structural concerns like sagging deck lines. Early detection of these warning signs is crucial to prevent extensive damage, increased repair costs, health risks from mold, and decreased property value. Don't delay in seeking professional inspection if you notice any of these indicators of roof deterioration.
